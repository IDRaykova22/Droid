Heipi

Heipi is a helpful robot designed to assist tourists in navigating their way out of hotels, motels, and other accommodations, especially when they don't speak the local language or are unfamiliar with the surroundings. Using a user-friendly interface, tourists can interact with him through voice commands or a touch screen display. They can ask for directions, request recommendations, or seek assistance with any travel-related inquiries. Heipi responds promptly, providing clear and concise information to help tourists navigate their way with ease.
