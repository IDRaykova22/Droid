Sunny

Sunny is an innovative Virtual Reality (VR) system designed to enhance learning in subjects like physics, chemistry, and biology within schools. By immersing students in virtual environments, Sunny enables them to explore and interact with complex scientific concepts in a captivating and engaging way. The system offers a dynamic and immersive learning experience, allowing students to visualize abstract concepts and witness their practical applications. Sunny encourages active participation, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills as students actively navigate through virtual scenarios.
